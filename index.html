⭐ Proactive Plant Care

Preventing disease is easier than treating it. Here are expert-recommended tips:

🌬️ Promote Good Airflow

Prune plants and give them space. Good air circulation helps leaves dry quickly and prevents fungal growth.

💧 Water Wisely

Water at the base of the plant, not on the leaves. Always water in the morning for best results.

🧹 Practice Good Sanitation

Remove and destroy any dead or diseased leaves. Clean and sterilize your pruning tools regularly.
